Inception

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows the story of Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters the dreams of others to steal their secrets. As he takes on one last job, the boundaries between reality and dreams blur, leading to a thrilling and captivating adventure.
